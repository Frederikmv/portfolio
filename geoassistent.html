GEO assistent

Abstract

Geocaching has since its beginning in 2000 become a largecommunity with currently 3 million geocaches in 190 differentcountries. The rise of this community has motivated people toexperience nature by tracking and locating geocaches with theGPS module in their smartphones. Unfortunately, this way ofgetting directions encumbers the experience since users mustconsult their smartphones for directions, thereby taking theirfocus away from nature. To solve this we present GEO As-sistant, a wearable device that guides the user in the directionof a chosen geocache using haptic feedback. This enables theuser to experience the nature.

GEO assistent

This shows the prototype GEO assistent which is meant to be worn around the waist with two vibrators placed at each hip to give haptic feedback to the user.

Technologies

The two main components used for this project were a ATMEGA328 Microcontroller and U-blox GPS reveiver NEO6M with an Antenna. A library called TinyGPS++ was used to calculate the correct directon to move in order to reach the destination.
